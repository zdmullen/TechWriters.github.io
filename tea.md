# Brew a Mug of Tea

**You need:**
* A mug
* A kettle or pot
* Tea

**Follow theses steps:**

1. Put teabag in mug.
2. Pour boiling water onto teabag.
3. Leave for 3-5 minutes.
4. Remove teabag.
5. (Optional) Add a small quantity of milk, then stir.
6. (Optional) Add 1-3 teaspoons of sugar, then stir.
7. Leave to cool for at least five minutes.  
   Your tea is ready.
